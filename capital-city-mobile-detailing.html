Founded in Columbus

Capital City, Columbus. Since 2014.

The first big job was a 38-foot Class A motorhome stored at a Dublin facility — a call we took in early 2014 when every other mobile detailer in the metro had passed on it. We bought a forced-action polisher and a water reclamation system that same month, drove to the facility, and spent two days on that coach. The owner referred us to a fleet manager in Hilliard before the job was done.

A decade later the rig has grown but the process hasn't changed. Every coating starts with a full machine polish. Every substrate — oxidized gelcoat, chrome-effect wrap, bare fiberglass — gets the prep time it demands. We don't charge a shop rate; we charge for the hours we put in.
