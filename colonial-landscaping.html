Since 2011

A crew that lives on these streets.

Founded in Columbus in 2011, Colonial Landscaping has spent over a decade serving Franklin County homeowners and businesses with dependable, quality-first service that earns long-term relationships.

What started as a small crew committed to doing right by our neighbors has grown into a full-service landscaping operation with deep roots in the Columbus community. We know these neighborhoods because we live and work here.

Our BBB Accreditation since January 2018 reflects what we've always believed: a handshake matters, your time is valuable, and every yard deserves a crew that takes pride in the work.
