Our Story

About Cinnaswirl

Cinnaswirl started with a love of real ingredients and a belief that a perfect cinnamon roll could make anyone's morning better. What began as weekend baking for family and neighbors in Powell quickly turned into something bigger — a small business built on honest recipes and a relentless commitment to quality.

Every recipe was developed from scratch in our own kitchen, tested dozens of times, and refined on the honest feedback of our community. We don't use mixes, we don't cut fat, and we don't compromise on ingredients just to protect margins.

We show up at Columbus-area farmers markets every weekend because we believe great food belongs in the community that inspired it. When you buy from Cinnaswirl, you're supporting a small family bakery that genuinely cares about what you're eating.
