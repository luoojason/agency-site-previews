Before You Call

What Marcus gets asked at the front door.

How do I get a quote from DreamView?
Fill out our contact form below, call us at (614) 285-5857, or email Dreamviewlandscapingllc@gmail.com. We'll schedule a free on-site visit and send you a written estimate within 24–48 hours. No pressure, no obligation.
Do you offer warranties on your work?
Yes — we offer warranties on hardscape installations including paver patios, retaining walls, walkways, and fire pits. Warranty terms are project-specific and outlined in your written agreement before work begins.
What areas do you serve?
We serve Columbus and surrounding areas — Hilliard, Grove City, Whitehall, Reynoldsburg, Westland, Franklinton, and South Side Columbus. Not sure if we reach you? Give us a call.
What's included in a seasonal cleanup?
Leaf and debris removal, bed edging, cutting back perennials, blowing out turf and hard surfaces, and hauling everything away. Spring cleanups include a property walk-through to flag any winter damage.
How long does a hardscape project take?
A standard paver patio (200–400 sq ft) typically takes 2–4 days once materials are on-site. Retaining walls run 1–3 days depending on length and height. We give you a realistic timeline in your estimate.
What payment methods do you accept?
Cash, check, and all major credit cards. For larger projects we collect a deposit at signing with balance due upon completion. We're happy to discuss payment scheduling on big hardscape jobs.
Is there a minimum visit size for lawn care?
Our standard minimum for mowing visits is $45, which covers most residential lots up to about 5,000 sq ft. Larger properties are priced by lot size — exact cost in your written estimate before you commit.
Do you offer recurring lawn care plans?
Yes — most of our lawn care customers are on recurring plans. Weekly, bi-weekly, and custom-schedule options. Recurring customers get priority scheduling and consistent crews who know your property.

Who We Are

Marcus and Derek.
Columbus-raised, since 2017.

Marcus Okafor started DreamView in 2017 after spending six seasons as a crew lead for a large commercial operation. He wanted to bring the same precision to residential properties on Columbus's South Side — the neighborhood where he grew up — without the upsell pressure that comes with corporate contracts.

Today it's still Marcus and his brother Derek running every job. One thing that came from those early Hilliard jobs in year one: Columbus clay is unforgiving when you rush a retaining wall base. Every wall they build now gets a full gravel drainage bed and geotextile fabric, regardless of what the homeowner sees in the bid. It's what makes the five-year warranty real, not aspirational.

Being named a Nextdoor Neighborhood Favorite in 2023 means a lot — but the thing Marcus still talks about is the Franklinton duplex owner who called back eight months after a lawn renovation to say the bare spots from the 2023 drought never came back. That's the bar they hold themselves to.
