The Story Behind the Food

Chaneice, in her own words.

Growing up on Columbus's east side, Chaneice spent every Sunday at her grandmother's stove — learning smothered oxtails, slow-cooked greens, and the patience it takes to make a room go quiet when the food lands. That table is where she learned that a meal is never just a meal.

She started taking paid bookings in 2018, catering a graduation party for 60 guests out of a church kitchen on Livingston Avenue. Word got around fast. Within a year she had her catering license, an Ohio Cottage Food certification, and a waiting list that made her quit her day job.

Today she sources direct from Ohio farms — Snowville Creamery dairy, Canter Hill chicken, whatever the season puts on Clintonville market tables — and takes every event from consult to cleanup herself. You hire Chaneice, you get Chaneice.
